Overnight Oats with Peanut Butter, Banana and Chocolate Split

Ingredients

  • oats
  • oatmilk (or other plant milk)
  • peanut butter, unsalted and unsweetened
  • agave (or other) syrup
  • dark chocolate
  • banana
  • soy yoghurt
  • cocoa powder, unsweetend
  • vanilla
  • flaxseed, rough-ground
  • dash of salt
  • opt. toppings: granola/nuts/anything crunchy...

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients except the dark chocoloate and the banana in a leakproof lunchbox, jar or other container.
  2. Roughly grate the dark chocolate. Carefully add the chocolate split to the oatmeal.
  3. Put the container in your fridge overnight or until the oats are soaked and soft.
  4. Take out the oatmeal and cut banana to slices. To serve, top oatmeal with fresh banana slices and opt. granola or nuts for extra crunch.
